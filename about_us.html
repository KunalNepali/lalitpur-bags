About Lalitpur Bags

Welcome to Lalitpur Bags, your trusted destination for quality bag repairs, custom creations, and handcrafted designs. Based in the heart of Lalitpur, we’re proud to serve students, travelers, commuters, and creators with reliable service and beautiful craftsmanship.

Whether it’s fixing your beloved backpack, customizing a leather purse, or crafting a delivery bag for your business, we treat every item like our own. We specialize in:

  • 🧵 Bag Repairs (Chain, Zip, Sewing)
  • 👜 Custom Leather & Fabric Bags
  • 📦 Delivery & Business Orders

With a passionate team and deep attention to detail, we’re committed to making your bags last longer — and look better.
